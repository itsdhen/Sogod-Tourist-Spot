Arianne’s Bonsai

Arianne's Bonsai, where centuries of tradition collide with the peace of Southern Leyte, discover the creativity of miniature nature. Each of the exquisitely built bonsai trees in this charming garden tells a unique tale of perseverance and accuracy. Arianne's Bonsai allows you to take in its distinct beauty, making it ideal for nature lovers, hobbyists, or anybody looking for a tranquil getaway.

La Caridad Farm

La Caridad Farm, a verdant haven that offers clean air, picturesque scenery, and a taste of sustainable farming, discover the allure of country living. Explore colourful landscapes, taste fresh produce from the farm, and discover the history of agriculture in the area. La Caridad Farm is a great spot to come back in touch with nature and is ideal for families, eco-tourists, and anybody else looking for a cool retreat.

Café Romara

Indulge in the perfect blend of relaxation and flavor at Café Romara, a cozy spot nestled in Sogod’s serene countryside. Savor expertly brewed coffee, delectable pastries, and hearty meals while enjoying breathtaking views. Whether you’re catching up with friends, working remotely, or simply unwinding, Café Romara offers the ideal ambiance to make every moment special.
